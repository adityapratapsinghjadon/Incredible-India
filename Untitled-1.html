India is known for its rich heritage and culture and along with it, the country is blessed with many beautiful and exotic places that are not found anywhere else in the world. The mountains, the hills, the beaches, the islands, the temples, they all make the country an Incredible India. Here you can check most famous tourist places in India.

Most foreigners visit India for a spiritual experience that the country offers. India is the land of pilgrimages, its the land of knowledge. Here, we are going to have a look at some of the most famous tourist places in India.

1. Agra, Uttar Pradesh

The city lies on the banks of river Yamuna and it is one of most famous tourist places in India due to the presence of the Taj Mahal and the rich heritage and culture of the place. The place is also known for marble artefacts, its food and other art and architecture. The place is also very populous and connectivity to other nearby areas is well maintained.

Ladakh is known as the land of high passes. Previously, it was part of Jammu and Kashmir but now it is a separately created union territory. The place is filled with beautiful mountains, snow deserts, Alpine trees, and many lakes which makes it one of the famous tourist places in India.

4. Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Shimla is one of the famous tourist places in India, renowned as the Queen of Hills. It is known for its snow-capped mountain peaks, Victorian-era architecture, bustling bazaars, stunning waterfalls, and more. As the capital of Himachal Pradesh, Shimla has a rich history and was once the summer capital of British India. Besides its natural beauty and historical significance, Shimla is also famous for its handicraft shops, wooden toys, and various other forms of crafts that can be found throughout the city.

7. Assam

Assam, a state located in northeastern India. There are seven popular tourist destinations in Assam, including Kaziranga National Park, Majuli Island, Kamakhya Temple, Manas National Park, Tawang, Sivasagar, and Hajo. Each destination is briefly described, highlighting their unique features such as wildlife, cultural heritage, and scenic beauty.

8. Jammu and Kashmir

Jammu and Kashmir, one of the famous tourist places in India, is a beautiful region located in the northernmost part of the country famous for its rich cultural heritage and history, with ancient temples, forts and more. Famous tourist attractions in Jammu and Kashmir include Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Leh, and Ladakh, which offer unique experiences for visitors of all interests.
